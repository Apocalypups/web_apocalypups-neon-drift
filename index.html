How it plays

Fight on the beat.

Apocalypups is a side-view rhythm brawler: every punch, dash, and pup-swap lives on a live BPM clock. Miss the pocket and the combo dies.

  1. 01

    Hit the window

    Perfect lands center-grid. Good still counts. Miss locks you out and resets the chain.

  2. 02

    Swap pups mid-combo

    Slayer, Fang, Bruce, Slash — each pup rewires reach, timing, and how the beat feels under your thumbs.

  3. 03

    Boxer-step the lane

    Micro-dashes forward and back. Close distance on the snare, retreat on the off-beat, dust on the asphalt.

  4. 04

    Drop Showtime

    Charge the meter on clean hits. Flip Halftime slow-mo, neon zoom, then unload before scavengers remember how to block.
